About Me

As a first generation immigrant, I saw what it took for my parents to leave their families and old lives in Israel behind to build something new here. Driven by the “American Dream”, they flourished and succeeded, allowing my brothers and I to grow up in an environment that they did not have. As I have grown, I’ve tried to emulate this same sense of hard-work and passion in what I do, especially during my time with V-Armed, a virtual reality combat simulation company, and the various projects that I work on in my free time. Alongside this, I am also an avid sports fan, with my favorite sport being basketball, as I am a life-long fan of the San Antonio Spurs. I also enjoy football (Go Giants), tennis, volleyball, and soccer. However, I also greatly enjoy reading and writing. As a computer/data science double major, taking the time to journal or read a few pages of a book are great ways to reset and relax.
